background

Sky High is the culmination of 22 years of extensive research across India, Iceland, and the UK, with a specific emphasis on Indian educational contexts.

Backed by a 65-year family legacy of managing the world's largest school, recognized by Guinness World Records and awarded the UNESCO Prize for Peace Education

The ALfA pedagogy, introduced in 2022 with the endorsement of India's Ministry of Education, demonstrated success in City Montessori School pilots, prompting further adoption.

At the heart of education's transformation is holistic and all-rounded development of every child, building their character and inspiring them to become good citizens.

ALfA Sky High

Dear Esteemed Educators,

Envision a transformative education that was once just a distant dream, now within every parent's budget, and by which children can master a year's curriculum in half a year, joyfully.

Our 22 years multi-country research shows children learn more when they collaborate. With books and processes designed for pair work, ALfA (Accelerating Learning for All) is a child-led approach by which children master content at least one grade higher and develop 21st century skills and traits naturally.

We cannot blend the old with the new, just as you cannot add smartphone features to a traditional landline. Like a smart phone with advanced features, we can truly grasp ALfA by using it.

ALfA has gained recognition from the Harvard Graduate School of Education, USA. What,s more! 100% of the proceeds go to DEVI Sanstan,s literacy programs, with exponential expansion into 14,000 government schools in just a year.

We're inviting institutions ready for a significant change to embrace the future of education, to move forward with faith. Let,s begin a much needed new chapter in education today.
